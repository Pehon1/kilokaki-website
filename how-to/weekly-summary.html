How to Read Your Weekly Summary

KiloKaki sends a weekly summary every Sunday. Here's what the numbers actually tell you.

Your weekly summary is your macro report card. It shows what you ate over the past 7 days compared to your daily targets — so you can see patterns, not just single meals.

How to Get It

KiloKaki sends your weekly summary automatically every Sunday at 10am SGT.

What the Summary Shows

1

Total logs

How many times you logged food this week. More logs = more data = better insights.

2

Most logged food

The food item you logged most frequently this week. Reveals your eating patterns.

3

Average calories per logging day

Your total calories divided by the number of days you actually logged food. This tells you your typical daily intake on active days.

4

Weekly trend

How this week's logging compares to last week — more logs, fewer logs, or about the same.

💡 Consistency matters more than perfection. Even logging 3-4 days a week gives you valuable trend data. Don't stress about missed days.

Managing Your Weekly Summary

  • Turn off: Type /weekly off
  • Turn on: Type /weekly on
