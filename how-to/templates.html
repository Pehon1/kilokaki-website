How to Use Meal Templates

Save your go-to meals. Log your usual lunch in seconds — one tap.

If you eat the same things regularly, meal templates save you from typing them out every single time. Set it up once, use it forever.

Creating a Template

1

Log the meal normally first

Use voice, text, or photo to log the meal exactly as you would normally.

2

Tap the 💾 Save as template button

After KiloKaki logs your meal, you'll see a "💾 Save as template" button below the entry. Tap it.

The template is saved immediately with the food name. You can optionally rename it with the ✏️ Rename button that appears.

3

KiloKaki confirms

"✅ [food] saved as template! Use /log to quickly log."

Using a Template

Type /log to see your saved templates as buttons. Tap one to log it instantly.

You can also type /templates to see all your templates — tap any one to log it, or tap ❌ Delete to remove it.

💡 Templates are perfect for: work-day lunches, gym-day breakfasts, post-workout shakes, weekly meal prep. You can have up to 10 templates.

Managing Templates

  • List: Type /templates to see all saved templates
  • Use: Tap a template button to log it instantly
  • Rename: After saving, tap ✏️ Rename, then type the new name
  • Delete: In /templates, tap ❌ Delete next to the template
  • Update: Log the new version, then tap 💾 Save as template (it creates a new one — delete the old)
