How to Edit or Delete a Log Entry

Made a mistake? Fix it — KiloKaki updates your daily totals automatically.

Logging is supposed to be low-friction — and that includes fixing mistakes. You can edit the food item, adjust quantities, or remove an entry entirely.

Editing a Log Entry

Reply directly to the log entry you want to change:

Reply to a log entry "change to chicken breast 200g"
Reply to a log entry "should be 1.5 servings not 2"
Reply to a log entry "replace with grilled fish instead"

KiloKaki replaces the old entry with the new one and recalculates your daily macro totals.

Deleting a Log Entry

Reply to the entry and tell KiloKaki to remove it:

Reply to a log entry "delete this" or "remove entry"

The entry disappears, your daily totals update, and that's it.

Common Fixes

  • Wrong food: "change to salmon not tuna"
  • Wrong quantity: "was 1.5 bowls not 2"
  • Double logged: "delete this duplicate"
  • Logged to wrong meal slot: "move to breakfast" or adjust the date
💡 Your daily totals recalculate the moment you edit or delete. No manual math — KiloKaki handles the numbers.
