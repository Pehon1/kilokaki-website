How to Adjust Portions

Tell KiloKaki how many servings you ate. It does the math.

Nutrition databases give you macro values per 100g or per serving. KiloKaki lets you scale those values to match exactly how much you ate — no spreadsheet required.

Step by Step

1

Log the food as usual

Send a voice note, photo, or text — whatever method you prefer. KiloKaki returns a macro estimate based on a standard portion.

2

Tap the "Edit" button below the log entry

After KiloKaki logs your meal, you'll see an Edit button (or inline keyboard) right below the entry.

3

Adjust the portion or macros directly

Change the serving multiplier (e.g. 1 → 2.5) or enter custom macro values. KiloKaki recalculates everything and updates the entry.

💡 You can adjust portions even after the fact — just find the log entry and tap Edit. Your daily totals update immediately.

What You Can Adjust

  • Serving multiplier — 1.5x, 2x, 0.5x, etc.
  • Calories, protein, carbs, fat — enter exact values if you know them
  • Sodium — track salt intake if you're watching it
